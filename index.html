Pig Game

Pig game

Play with a friend!

Players take turns to roll a single die as many times as they wish.

Each roll is added to their current score and the player's turn continues.

If the player rolls a 1, they score nothing and will be the next player's turn.

If a player chooses to "hold", their current score is added to their total points and will be the next player's turn.

The first player to score 100 or more points wins.
